04 / Why Now

The timing is
structural, not hype

01

Model velocity exceeds hardware cycles

AI models are changing faster than manual hardware architecture cycles can follow — creating a growing mismatch.

02

Edge and specialized inference

Edge and specialized inference create stronger demand for custom silicon that general-purpose chips can't serve.

03

Ecosystem investment

CHIPS Act, DoD, and semiconductor ecosystem investment make new infrastructure layers more fundable than ever.

04

Design complexity demands automation

AI-era design complexity increases the need for model-level hardware automation — manual approaches no longer scale.

These are structural forces, not trends — they make model-to-hardware automation an infrastructure inevitability.
